    \hypertarget{biot-savart-law-i}{%
\section{Biot Savart Law I:}\label{biot-savart-law-i}}

\hypertarget{on-axis-field-of-a-current-loop}{%
\subsection{On-axis field of a current
loop}\label{on-axis-field-of-a-current-loop}}

In this exercise, you will be computing the magnetic field for a wire
loop of radius \(R\) carrying current \(I\), both on the axis of
symmetry and off the axis of symmetry. The former can be computed
analytically with relative ease, and although the latter can also, the
solution involves the complete elliptic functions of the first and
second kind (you'll solve this in graduate school should you choose to
continue your study of physics).

    Estimated time to complete: 2-4 hours

    \hypertarget{theory-derivation-of-the-on-axis-field}{%
\subsection{Theory: Derivation of the on-axis
field}\label{theory-derivation-of-the-on-axis-field}}

For the first part of this exercise, we want you to use the Biot-Savart
